Designed to connect student founders and those willing to join a startup at NYU.

Founders can post opportunities such as: Looking for a co‑founder, Startup internships, Part‑time roles in student‑led ventures.

Why NYU Startup Connect

Currently, NYU students seeking to explore opportunities in startups have two ways: Spreadsheets (like the Stern internship list) and Networking Events (hosted by Leslie or other NYU clubs). There is currently no website where NYU students can both post and apply for startup roles and search for co‑founders.
